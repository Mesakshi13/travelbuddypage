KASOL

Kasol is a hamlet in kullu district of Himachal Pradesh. A wonder to behold ,with an air which not only mystifies your sense but makes you live in the impulses of life, it is a village mothered by Parvati Valley. It is best known for its thrilling treks, flavoursome food, roaring river and heartfelt humanity.

HAMPI

Hampi is an ancient village of karnataka. It's dotted with numerous ruined temple complexes from the Vijayanagara Empire. It is declared as a World Heritage Site. The temples of Hampi, it's monolithic sculptures and monuments, attract the traveler because of their excellent workmanship.

MCLEODGANJ

McleodGanj is a suburb in kangra district of Himachal Pradesh. It is known for its monastries, museums, temples , waterfalls , lakes and Tibetian settlements. Some interesting things to do in Mcleodganj is Trekking. Best time to visit is between October-March. A must visit place in your budget.
